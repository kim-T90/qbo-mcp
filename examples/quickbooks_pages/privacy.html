QuickBooks integration privacy policy

This page describes how YOUR ORGANIZATION uses QuickBooks Online data for its internal integration and automation tooling.

What data we access

We access QuickBooks Online accounting data that our organization has authorized for internal operational use. This may include company information, chart of accounts, customers, vendors, items, invoices, bills, payments, and reports.

How we use the data

  • To support internal workflows, reporting, and accounting operations.
  • To allow authorized team members and internal tools to query QuickBooks data.
  • To create or update accounting records when explicitly authorized by the business.

Storage and access

Access is limited to authorized personnel and systems operated by YOUR ORGANIZATION. OAuth credentials and refresh tokens are stored only for the purpose of maintaining the approved QuickBooks connection.

Contact

If you have questions about this integration or your data, contact finance@example.com.
